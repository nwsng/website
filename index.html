What We Believe

Our Vision

One Church, One Faith, One Spirit—empowering the next generations to sing a new song (Psalm 96:1-3).

We believe God is calling us to something fresh and vibrant. This isn't about doing church the same old way—it's about creating a space where the next generations can discover what it means to follow Jesus in their own unique way. When we talk about "one church, one faith, one Spirit," we're talking about unity that transcends our differences. We're talking about a community where everyone belongs, where everyone has a voice, and where everyone can contribute to what God is doing. The new song we're singing isn't just about music—it's about a new way of being the church, a new expression of faith that speaks to where people are today while staying rooted in the timeless truth of the Gospel.

Our Mission

Fostering a Spirit-led community where the young generations grow in faith, live out the Gospel, and bring Christ's presence into everyday life (Acts 2:42-47).

Our mission is really about creating a place where faith becomes real and practical. We're not interested in just showing up on Sundays and checking a box. We want to see people actually grow—not just in knowledge, but in their relationship with Jesus. That means we're committed to teaching God's Word in ways that make sense, creating spaces for honest conversation, and walking alongside each other through the ups and downs of life. When we say "Spirit-led," we mean we're trying to follow where God is leading, not just doing what we've always done. And bringing Christ's presence into everyday life? That's about helping people see that following Jesus isn't just a Sunday thing—it's a Monday morning, Tuesday afternoon, Wednesday evening thing. It's about how we work, how we love, how we serve, and how we live.

Our Team

Samuel Kim

Lead Pastor, Rev. (DMin)

Rev. Samuel Kim founded New Song Church with many years of pastoral ministry experience, having served congregations in Mississauga, Hawaii, California, Maryland, and Missouri.

He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Christian Education, a Master of Theology, a Master of Divinity in Youth, Family, and Culture, and a Doctor of Ministry in Non-Profit Organizational Leadership.

Pastor Samuel carries a deep passion for building the church with and for the next generation, equipping leaders, and cultivating a Christ-centered community that worships, serves, and grows together.

Peter Jang

Director, Worship & Creative Arts

Peter joined New Song Church as part of the founding team, bringing extensive experience in worship ministry, creative arts, and technology. For many years, he has served as a worship drummer with worship teams across the Greater Toronto Area.

Professionally, he is a tech lead in the technology industry, specializing in research and development in AI and energy systems.

With a heart for worship and innovation, Peter is passionate about creating meaningful, God-honoring worship experiences that engage both the church and the wider community.
